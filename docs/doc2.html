Your User Account

Signing Up

You can create a new account with Dreamr by tapping the "Sign Up" button on the log in screen. To sign up you must provide your desired username, an email address to associate with the account and a secure password.

Logging In

To log in type the email address and password that you signed up with into the "Log In" screen and tap "Log In". You will be presented with the dashboard, which you can find out more in the next section.

Viewing your profile

You can view information about your profile by tapping the menu icon at the top right of the dashboard and selecting "My Account". Here you can view information such as the number of dreams you have posted and the types of dreams they were. You can also view when your account was created.

Logging Out

To log out, tap the menu icon at the top right of the screen and tap "Log Out".
