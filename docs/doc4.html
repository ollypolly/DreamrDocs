Interacting with Dreams

Posting

To post a new dream simply tap the "+" (Plus) icon at the bottom right corner of the screen. This will bring up a screen in which you can enter the title of the dream, a short description and various information about the dream including whether it was lucid, recurring or a nightmare.

You can check the box titled "Post to Dreamscape" to post your dream in the Dreamscape or Nightscape. This can be undone later by editing the dream if needed.

If you are too tired to type when remembering your dream you can also use the "Speech to text" function to say your dream outloud.

Viewing

To view a dream, tap on the desired dream. A pop up box will be displayed showing more information about the dream. If this dream belongs to you you will be able to edit or change its Dream/Nightscape status.

Editing

To edit a dream that you have posted, select the dream from "My Dreams" and tap "Edit Dream". From here you can edit the text in the boxes or adjust the sliders as neccesary. You may also decide to remove or post the dream to Dream/Nightscape. Once you are finished editing, tap "Save".
